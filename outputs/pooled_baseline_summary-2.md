# Pooled baseline summary

The pooled baseline uses 282 complete country-year rows from 30 countries.
The model includes year indicators and clustered standard errors by country. The R-squared is 0.221.

Main coefficient patterns:
- `log_gdp_per_capita` has a negative association. The estimate is -0.546.
- `unemployment_rate_pc` has a positive association. The estimate is 0.160.
- `poverty_or_social_exclusion_pc` has a positive association. The estimate is 0.062.
- `government_health_expenditure_gdp_pc` has a positive association. The estimate is 0.159.
- `compulsory_health_financing_gdp_pc` has a negative association. The estimate is -0.401.

The coefficients describe aggregate associations in the complete-case Eurostat panel.
They should not be read as evidence about cause and result.
